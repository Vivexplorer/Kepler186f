Kepler-186f: A Brief Overview

Kepler-186f is an Earth-sized exoplanet orbiting the red dwarf star Kepler-186, located about 582 light-years away in the constellation Cygnus. Discovered by NASA's Kepler spacecraft in 2014, this exoplanet is of significant interest to scientists due to its potential habitability.

Characteristics of Kepler-186f:

  • Discovered: April 17, 2014
  • Size: Similar to Earth
  • Potential Habitability: Kepler-186f is located in the habitable zone of its star, where conditions might be suitable for liquid water to exist.
